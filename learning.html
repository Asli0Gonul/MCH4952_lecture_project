Learning paths

Learn Underwater Robotics Step by Step

Follow a structured roadmap from fundamentals to ROV system integration.

01

ROV Fundamentals

Understand ROV types, buoyancy, stability, tethering, and underwater operating conditions.

02

Mechanical Design

Learn frame design, pressure considerations, material selection, and modular component mounting.

03

Electronics & Power

Study batteries, regulators, ESCs, thruster wiring, waterproof connectors, and protection circuits.

04

Control & Software

Explore manual control, feedback loops, ROS-based systems, perception, and autonomy basics.
