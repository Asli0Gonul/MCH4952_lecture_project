The Problem

Underwater robotics requires multidisciplinary knowledge, but information is often scattered across papers, forums, courses, calculators, and vendor websites.

  • Mechanical, electronics, control, and communication knowledge are fragmented.
  • Students spend time switching between unrelated platforms.
  • Engineering calculators are not always accessible for early-stage design.
  • ROV and marine robotics career paths are difficult to discover.

Our Solution

Deep Dive Robotics brings learning, design, research, and career discovery into one accessible platform.

  • Engineering tools accessible in 2–3 clicks.
  • Research paper finder with topic-based filters.
  • ROV and marine robotics job board.
  • Clear learning paths for students and early-stage engineers.
